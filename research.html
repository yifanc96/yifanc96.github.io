Research

My research is motivated by the heterogeneous and high-dimensional analysis and computational challenges present in applied mathematics, statistics, and machine learning. My work centers on utilizing analytical and numerical techniques from multiscale analysis, stochastic processes, and randomized algorithms to tackle these challenges. Specific applications include partial differential equations, inverse problems, and data science problems in computational engineering, materials science, and imaging.

Some specific topics that I have worked on:

  • Multiscale, randomized algorithms: numerical homogenization, randomized numerical linear algebra
  • Gaussian processes, kernel methods: scalable algorithms, uncertainty quantification, learning theory
  • Stochastic, dynamical sampling: Monte Carlo, gradient flows, optimal transport, generative modeling
